# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** [04/14/2926]

Doc Scanner ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our app handles your information.

## 1. Information We Collect

We do **not collect, store, or share any personal data** from users.

All documents, images, and data scanned using the app are processed **locally on your device** and are not uploaded to any server.

## 2. Document Scanning & Storage

* Scanned documents and images are saved **only on your device**
* We do not access, view, or store your files externally
* You have full control over your data

## 3. QR Code Scanning

The QR code scanner works entirely on-device.
No scanned data is stored or transmitted unless you choose to open or share it.

## 4. Text Extraction (OCR)

Text extraction is performed locally on your device.
We do not store or transmit extracted text.

## 5. Permissions

The app may request the following permissions:

* **Camera** – to scan documents and QR codes
* **Storage** – to save scanned files on your device

These permissions are used only for core functionality.

## 6. Data Security

Since all processing is done locally, your data remains private and secure on your device.

## 7. Third-Party Services

This app does not use third-party services that collect user data.

## 8. Children’s Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13.
We do not knowingly collect any personal information from children.

Any changes will be posted on this page.
